# UniCrypt Platform Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic milestones for UniCrypt, shifting from a prototype university application portal to a robust, decentralized **Credential Intelligence Platform**.

## Milestone 1: Decentralized Foundation (Current)
- **Role Isolation**: Separation of Student, Institution, and Platform Administrator scopes and AI contexts.
- **Client-Side Tool Orchestration**: Event-driven custom actions for document viewing, comparison, and vault access.
- **Vault Pre-Search Resolver**: Context-aware credential matching inside conversation streams.

## Milestone 2: Automated Pipelines & Vision Outcomes (Upcoming)
- **Enhanced Vision Extracts**: Optical Character Recognition (OCR) validation with confidence indices.
- **Background Sync Cache**: Caching organization metrics (e.g., acceptance rates, tuition details) locally to minimize Firestore reads under Firebase Spark plans.
- **Custom Verification Checklists**: Dynamic template mapping for employment, licensing, and immigration workflows.

## Milestone 3: Cryptographic Signatures & Compliance
- **Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs)**: Issue and verify credentials cryptographically.
- **Federated Governance Rules**: Automated verification rulesets matching international education standards (WES, etc.).
